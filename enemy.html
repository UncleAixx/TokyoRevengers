Tetta Kisaki is the main antagonist of Tokyo Revengers up until his death after the Kanto Incident.
Kisaki is a ruthless, cunning, and ambitious individual who will stop at nothing to achieve his goals. Although average in stature, his power to rule with fear makes even those stronger than him obey him.
